What is the EBITD Amargin calculator?
The EBITDA margin calculator is a tool that determines how operational efficient a company is.
How to calculate EBITD Amargin?
To calculate EBITD Amargin you should use the following formula:
EBITD Amargin = (EBITDA / total revenues) * 100
